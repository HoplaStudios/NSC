How Do I Join NSC?

1. You need to be a non-swearer.

2. Go to the Apply/Enroll page.

3. You can also E-Mail any member with ranks [Admin] or [PXP].

Can Swearers Join?

One word: NO

What If I Swore In The Past And Now I want To Join?

Well the team will have to investigate and if you haven't swore recently

and you promise not to swear again the team (which is made up of [Admin]s and [PXP]s)

will THINK to give you an exception but expectations are strict so its a 50% 50%.
